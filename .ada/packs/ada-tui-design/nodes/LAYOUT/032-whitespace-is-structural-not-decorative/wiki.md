# LAYOUT.032 · Whitespace is structural, not decorative

> ∵ source · L2 · C2 · area **LAYOUT** · from `src/tui/ink/Welcome.ts`

## Summary
One blank line between logical blocks, 1–2 cells padding inside any framed box, a fixed spacing rhythm held everywhere (Welcome.ts paddingX:2 / explicit gap rows). Never 0-padding (cramped), never random 1-vs-2 newline mixing.

## Why it matters
Density is the absence of decorative noise, not the absence of space; rhythm is what makes a dense screen still feel calm.

## Failure if missing
Inconsistent gaps make the screen feel hand-assembled and cheap, even with a perfect palette.
