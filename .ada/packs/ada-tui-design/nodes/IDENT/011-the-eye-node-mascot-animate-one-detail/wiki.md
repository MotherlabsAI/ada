## Summary
The default mascot is an eye (art.ts DEFAULT_MASCOT='eye') — groundable to Ada's function: a context compiler that watches your graph, not arbitrary decoration. Rows 1 and 3 are byte-identical to the blink frame; only the eye row changes (◉→═), so the silhouette is stable and flicker-free.

## Why it matters
Personality from a single moving detail is the cheapest, calmest way to feel 'a little alive' without violating the one-moving-thing rule.

## Failure if missing
A fully animated mascot competes with the cursor and the compile heartbeat — three moving things, the opposite of calm.
