# UNK.101 · Mouse / OSC-8 hyperlinks — adopt or stay keyboard-pure?

> Ω residue · L1 · C0 · area **UNK** · from `SEED (root intent)`

## Summary
Modern terminals support mouse events and OSC-8 hyperlinks (clickable node ids, file paths to the pack on disk). Whether Ada adopts any pointer affordance or stays deliberately keyboard-pure is an open identity decision, not just a feature toggle.

## Why it matters
Clickable pack paths could make A5 (filesystem-backed) tangible; mouse could also dilute the keyboard-first identity.

## Failure if missing
Either a half-mouse surface that works in some terminals and not others, or a missed cheap win (clickable .ada/ paths).
