# MOTION.042 · One welcome breath — border OR mascot, never both

> ∵ source · L2 · C2 · area **MOTION** · from `src/tui/ink/Welcome.ts`

## Summary
A slow breath on EITHER the banner gradient OR the mascot eye-blink — never two breathing elements at once. Welcome.ts runs the gradient ramp (~250ms) + a single eye blink (open ~3.8s/shut ~140ms); kill all of it the instant view flips to graph.

## Why it matters
Two competing breaths read as nervousness; one slow breath reads as calm presence.

## Failure if missing
Border and mascot pulse simultaneously and the home screen feels anxious instead of inviting.
