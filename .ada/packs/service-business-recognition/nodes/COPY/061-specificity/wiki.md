# ◇ ● ∴ κ ⇒ COPY.061 — Specificity

## ⟡ Summary
Specificity is the cheapest credibility multiplier on a service-business page and the one operators reflexively discard: "on-site within 90 minutes, 4,200 jobs across Kelowna since 2009" beats "fast, reliable service" on all three readers at once. Concrete numbers, named neighbourhoods, dated facts, and exact prices (1) flip a skeptical human from puffery-discount to verify-mode, (2) hand Google and answer engines extractable entities/quantities for long-tail relevance, and (3) leave the adjective-fog competitor un-citable. The non-obvious frontier point: in the query fan-out / passage-retrieval regime, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and AI Overviews preferentially lift self-contained sentences carrying a concrete attributable fact ("responds in 90 minutes", "serves these 6 areas") over abstraction — so specificity is now simultaneously a conversion lever AND an agent-recognition lever, and the SAME fact-bearing sentence is what gets snippet-extracted. This compiles to hard discipline: a banned-abstraction lexicon (quality/trust/professional/best/reliable) that lints every claim in heading position, and quantified-claim slots (response time, job count, years, # areas, price-from) Claude must fill with REAL supplied facts — never inventing a number (Axiom A2), flagging any unfillable slot as an Ω residue gap. The honest-specificity trap most guides miss: trivially-specific-but-meaningless ("uses 3-step process") passes a regex but fails the differentiation rubric, and the highest-leverage slot — exact published prices — is the one trades operators most resist, so it must be surfaced as an explicit decision, not assumed.

## ∴ Why it matters
Specificity is the trait that lets the same sentence persuade a human, get indexed for a long-tail query, and get lifted by an answer engine. Vagueness fails all three readers simultaneously and is the default failure mode of generated and lazy copy — directly threatening 'ranks', 'recognized by agents', and 'converts'.

## ! Failure if missing
Copy collapses into interchangeable adjective-fog: humans discount it as marketing puff, Google finds no distinguishing entities for long-tail relevance, and LLMs skip past it for a competitor who stated a concrete fact. The business becomes one undifferentiated 'quality local provider' among hundreds.
