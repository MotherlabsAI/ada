# ◇ ● ∴ ⇒ COPY.070 — Risk Framing

- cluster: COPY · depth: L4 · truth: ∴ inference
- checkability: C2 (rubric/LLM)
- compiles to: graph, wiki, c, claude, blueprint

**Summary.** For a local service, the booking decision is governed by perceived RISK, not perceived benefit — the buyer's live question is not "how good?" but "what if this goes wrong, costs more than quoted, or wastes my time?" Copy that explicitly transfers that risk from buyer to business converts disproportionately. The trades-specific levers: a free no-obligation estimate (kills the "what if it's expensive just to find out" fear), a named guarantee with a SPECIFIC remedy ("on time or we waive the call-out fee", "fixed-price quote, no surprises"), and upfront pricing that pre-empts being overcharged. Three non-obvious moves: (1) a guarantee is a revenue strategy, not a refund policy — the rare remedy payout is dwarfed by the conversion lift, so price it as marketing spend, not liability; (2) a concrete remedy ("double your money back", "we waive the fee") vastly outperforms vague "satisfaction guaranteed" because loss-aversion only fires on a quantified, claimable loss the buyer can picture; (3) the strongest frame inverts loss-aversion onto inaction — make NOT booking the riskier choice (the leak keeps rotting the subfloor; the problem compounds), never via fake countdowns. Hard constraint: every promise is an operational obligation the executor and business must keep, so the compiler emits risk-reversal copy ONLY for guarantees the business has confirmed — unconfirmed guarantees become residue, never invented.

**Why.** Risk is the last barrier between a persuaded local buyer and a booking. Explicit, specific risk-reversal is one of the highest-lift copy moves available and is nearly absent from generic competitor pages, making it both a conversion lever and a differentiator. It directly addresses 'converts attention into bookings'.

**Failure if missing.** The visitor stays one step back ('I'll get a few more quotes first', 'I don't want to pay just to be told the problem') because the page left all the risk on their side. Persuaded traffic defers indefinitely, and deferral is a lost booking.
