# Domain Glossary



**Staff Member** — A person who delivers services and owns a calendar of bookings.

**Client / Customer** — A person who books and pays for services.

**Service Offering** — Something bookable, with a duration and a price.

**Appointment** — A booked slot: a client, a staff member, a service, a start and end time, and a lifecycle state.

**Deposit** — An up-front partial payment that holds a booking.

**Payment** — Money moving in or out, recorded in the ledger in integer minor units.
