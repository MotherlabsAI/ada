# Workflows



### WORKFLOW.005 — Booking Flow
Select service -> choose staff and slot -> confirm -> hold with deposit. The flow the double-booking check guards.

### WORKFLOW.006 — Deposit Flow
Capture a deposit at booking time and reconcile it at checkout.

### WORKFLOW.007 — Reschedule Flow
Move a booking to a new slot without losing the deposit or creating an overlap.

### WORKFLOW.008 — Cancellation Flow
Release a slot and apply the cancellation policy to the deposit.
